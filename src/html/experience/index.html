A UX Engineer at Nest, an Alphabet company, I graduated in May of 2013 from Graceland University in Lamoni, IA where I majored in Computer Science/­Infor­mation Tech­nology and Math­ematics with a minor in Web Design.

Nest

2013 - Present: NEST

After graduating in May 2013, I drove out to California and began an internship at Nest Labs, a medium-sized startup in Palo Alto. I guess I made a good impression because they hired me. Since then, I have built a range of web based ...things. From support widgets, to product pages on nest.com.

Principal Financial Group

2012 Internship

When in interned for Principal Financial Group in the summer of 2012, I worked with a team of Java engineers to build a robust system to handle their secure documents delivery. Largely my work focused on building tests for their foundational transfer processes.

Smith & Associates

2011 Internship

Interning for Smith & Associates over the summer of 2011 was very educational. Most of my summer in Houston was spent as QA for all of their internal documenting tools.

Anders Crane Service

My whole life

Many are surprised that I have a long background of working around cranes. Though I am not a certified crane operator, I grew up running the crane in the family business, Anders Crane Service.

Graceland University

2008-2013

At Graceland University, I worked at the movie theater, where I eventually became the manager before graduating, and for STA+, where I was a part of a team that delivered technical support for both students and faculty.
